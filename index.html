About Our University

Accessible University is a pioneer in inclusive education, committed to providing world-class learning opportunities for all students, regardless of their abilities. Founded in 1985, we have consistently championed accessibility and universal design in all aspects of campus life.

Our mission is to foster an environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We believe that diversity in learning styles and abilities enriches the educational experience for everyone.

  • Inclusive Learning: All courses designed with accessibility at the forefront
  • Assistive Technology: State-of-the-art tools available campus-wide
  • Diverse Community: Students from over 80 countries
  • Personalized Support: Dedicated accessibility services team

Admissions

Join Our Community

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds and abilities. Our admissions process is designed to be accessible and accommodating.

Application Process

  1. Submit Online Application

    Our application portal is fully accessible with screen reader support and keyboard navigation.

  2. Request Accommodations

    Let us know if you need any accommodations during the application or interview process.

  3. Review Process

    Applications are reviewed holistically, considering academic achievement, personal qualities, and potential.

  4. Decision Notification

    Receive your decision via accessible email or your preferred communication method.
